The Journey

From emergency rooms to energy medicine. From saving lives in crisis to preventing crisis altogether.

The Beginning: Emergency Medicine

I graduated from Charles R. Drew University in 2009 as a Physician Assistant, ready to save lives. For a decade, I worked in emergency medicine—the front lines of healthcare where every second matters and protocols are king.

Those years taught me how to think fast, stay calm under pressure, and make life-or-death decisions. But they also showed me the limits of crisis intervention. We were brilliant at saving people from immediate death, but we weren't making them healthy.

The Wilderness Years: Alaska

In 2020, something called me north. I spent three years providing rural medicine in Alaska—isolated communities, limited resources, profound simplicity. The wilderness strips away everything nonessential. You learn what truly matters.

In that vast silence, between the northern lights and endless horizons, something shifted. I started asking different questions. Not "how do I treat this symptom?" but "why is this person sick in the first place?"

The Transformation: Yoga & Shamanic Wisdom

Parallel to my medical evolution, I discovered yoga—not as exercise, but as a complete system of healing and consciousness. Then came the Four Winds Society and shamanic training. Ancient wisdom traditions that Western medicine had dismissed as primitive suddenly revealed themselves as sophisticated technologies of healing.

I learned to work with energy, to see the body as intelligent rather than mechanical, to understand that healing happens when we remove obstacles rather than force solutions. The shaman doesn't heal—they create conditions for the body's innate wisdom to emerge.

The Realization

Modern medicine excels at emergency intervention. Ancient wisdom excels at root cause healing. The future of healthcare isn't choosing between them—it's integrating both.

The Birth of Gaia Functional Wellness

After Alaska, I returned to Miami and founded Gaia Functional Wellness—a practice that honors both my emergency medicine training and my shamanic initiation. We use cutting-edge functional medicine testing to identify root causes, and we treat the whole person: body, mind, energy, spirit.

We serve bilingual English/Vietnamese communities because healing shouldn't have language barriers. We offer three pathways—comprehensive functional medicine, telemedicine, and health coaching—because people need different levels of support at different times.

The Tech Side: Digital Dharma

But I'm not just a healer. I'm a systems thinker who sees patterns everywhere. The same principles that heal bodies can transform businesses. Remove obstacles. Honor natural intelligence. Create conditions for emergence rather than forcing outcomes.

I became fascinated with how technology—AI, blockchain, automation—can be used not to replace human connection, but to amplify it. How can we use digital tools to create more space for what actually heals? This became Digital Dharma.

I explore cryptocurrency and decentralized systems not for profit (though that's nice), but because they represent a fundamental shift in how we organize resources and power. I build AI automation not to eliminate jobs, but to eliminate soul-crushing repetitive work so humans can focus on what we do best: connect, create, heal.

The Vision: New Earth Infrastructure

Experiences like Burning Man showed me temporary autonomous zones where alternative ways of being are possible. Off-grid living research taught me about resilience and self-reliance. Functional medicine proves that the body can heal when given the right conditions.

I'm building infrastructure for a world that's emerging—healing sanctuaries that don't depend on broken systems, economic models that honor life instead of extracting it, technologies that serve consciousness instead of controlling it.

4Dharma: The Guiding Philosophy

Everything I do flows from this:

Invoke source healing power.
Remove what insults body, mind, and spirit.
Be good. Do good. Relieve suffering.

Where I Am Now

I'm a Physician Assistant running a functional medicine practice in Miami. I'm a shamanic practitioner trained in the Four Winds tradition. I'm a systems builder exploring AI, blockchain, and automation. I'm researching land for future healing sanctuaries. I'm a student of consciousness and a servant of healing.

I speak English and Vietnamese. I've completed Munay-Ki rites. I've attended Burning Man. I trade crypto. I automate phone systems and social media with AI. I run GI-MAP tests and prescribe supplements. I work with plant medicine wisdom and evidence-based protocols.

All of this isn't contradiction—it's integration. Ancient wisdom isn't opposed to modern innovation. They're two halves of a whole that was artificially split.

What I'm Building

For practitioners: Systems and training that let you focus on healing instead of administration.

For visionaries: Proof that another way is possible—medicine that heals, technology that serves, systems that regenerate.

For myself: A life that integrates all of who I am instead of choosing between medicine, spirituality, and technology.
